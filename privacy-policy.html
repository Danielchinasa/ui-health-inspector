Privacy Policy

UI Health Inspector  ·  Last updated: July 2026

Overview

UI Health Inspector is a Chrome Extension that scans web pages for UI, UX, and accessibility issues. We are committed to your privacy. This extension collects no personal data and sends no information to any external server.

Data Collection

UI Health Inspector does not collect, transmit, or sell any of the following:

  • Personally identifiable information (PII)
  • Browsing history or visited URLs
  • Page content, form inputs, or cookies
  • Crash reports or usage analytics

Local Storage

Scan results and user preferences (e.g. which scanners are enabled) are stored exclusively in Chrome's local extension storage (chrome.storage.local) on your own device. This data never leaves your browser and can be cleared at any time by removing the extension.

Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions, each used solely for its stated purpose:

  • activeTab – read the currently active tab so the scanner can inspect the page.
  • storage – save scan results and settings locally on your device.
  • scripting – inject the content script that runs the scanners on the page.
  • host_permissions (<all_urls>) – allow scanning any website you choose to scan. No data from visited sites is stored beyond the scan result you requested.

Third-Party Services

UI Health Inspector does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

This extension is a developer tool not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect any information from users under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

Contact
